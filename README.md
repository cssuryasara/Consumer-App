## About the App
Foodyz is an online food ordering app that makes the task of ordering food easier for the customers. It overcomes the disadvantages of the traditional queueing system. It is a medium to order online food hassle free from College Canteens. This system improves the method of taking the order from customer. The online food ordering system sets up a food menu online and customers can easily place the order as per their wish.
The payment can be made online or pay-on-delivery system.
